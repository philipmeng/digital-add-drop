Graduation Requirements

Core Requirements

Subject Requirement Notes
English 4 years ENG100, 200, 300 and three terms at 500-level
Mathematics Through MATH280 or 340 Completion of Math 280 or 340
World Languages 3 terms at 300-level or higher Or 1 term at 400-level or above, or completion of two different languages with specific conditions
Science Two 3-term lab sciences Most students complete Biology, Chemistry, and/or Physics
History History 100A and 100B, 201, and 300 Junior Year: HSS100A, HSS100B; Lower Year: HSS201; Upper Year: HSS300
Arts 4 terms (for 4-year students) Including 1 term of Art, 1 term of Music, plus 2 more of Art, Music, or Theatre and Dance
Philosophy & Religious Studies 1 term Usually taken in Lower year
Physical Education 1 term PHD200, usually taken in Lower year

Additional Requirements

  • Total Credits: 54 credits for 4-year students; 51 for 3-year; 48 for 2-year and 1-year students
  • Upper/Senior Years: 27 credits minimum across Upper and Senior years
  • Senior Year: 12 graded term credits minimum during Senior year
  • Swimming: Pass the 25-yard swim test
  • Standing: Must be in good standing (not on probation or suspension) at graduation

Course Load Policies

  • Junior/Lower: Five or six courses per term
  • Upper/Senior: Typically five courses per term
  • Four-Course Load: Seniors may take four courses under specific conditions if three are advanced
  • Six-Course Load: Approval is based on academic standing and department availability
